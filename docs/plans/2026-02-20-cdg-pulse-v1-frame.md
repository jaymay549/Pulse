# CDG Pulse V1 — Frame

## Source

> The project is called CDG Pulse. Its data is fed from a community of dealerships on WhatsApp, and then we show what members are saying about vendors in the groups (if they are pro tier members).
>
> The purpose is for users to be able to see insights about vendors before they sign up. Things like what people are saying about the vendor, what they like/dislike, find competitors to them, find new vendors period, etc.
>
> On the flip side, we also want to offer vendors the ability to be verified. This would grant them the ability to respond to reviews, have an improved "vendor profile" and more CTAs to convert our members into users, plus be able to gain insights about what people are saying about them and their competitors for deeper market intelligence and to find areas for attention.
>
> Let's brainstorm what we need for this to work as a V1 versus what we already have.

---

## Problem

Member vendor opinions are locked in WhatsApp chat. There's no structured way for prospective buyers to surface that intelligence when evaluating vendors. Vendors have no way to participate in their own narrative or understand what the market is saying about them and their competitors.

## Outcome

A V1 where:
- Members can research vendors using real peer insights, discover alternatives, and make better buying decisions
- Vendors can claim their profile, respond to reviews, and get structured market intelligence — enough value to justify a paid subscription
